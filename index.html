About Me


Aaron was born in San Jose, California to a pair of swashbuckling pirates, whom after a brief stint searching for buried treasure ended up moving and posing as medical cowboys in Dallas, Texas. Following high school, Aaron ended up marooned at New York University studying Film and Television. Upon graduation Aaron became the subject of a witch hunt on account of a misunderstanding about the wealth gap in America, he was driven out of town by a large, elderly bourgeouis cult thriving around Gramercy Park...

He retreated to Paris for a few months with nothing but his backpack and acoustic guitar, following in the footsteps of Paul Simon. When he returned to the United States with nowhere to turn, Aaron found refuge hopping aboard a train which was smuggling bell-bottoms, bourbon and hair to Nashville. Aaron made fast friends with a group of musicians at a commune in an abondoned frathouse by the Auto Diesel College in East Nashville. He toured around under the pseudoname Merc, quickly gaining fame playing in a 60's tinted rock group named The Bumbs. After a couple years The Bumbs and the communal home fell apart due to the bad management under the aggressive antics of a 3-legged dog named Bonzo. Penniless and misguided, Aaron fled to a recording studio deep in the ghetto of North Nashville.

He isolated himself for months-- saving money while recording ramblin' misguided hip-hop soul artists. Luckily, Aaron ended up in a love affair with the magic squirrel of Piedmont Park during a tour stop in Atlanta. The magical squirrel promptly cast a spell on Aaron allowing him to access a computer programming/web developer bootcamp at Georgia Tech where he now is rumored to still be...

And there you have it- the completely true history of Aaron Endelman.
